The Silly Billy Bubbles Tour!

Silly Billy will pop up in bubbles to give a tour of the app/website!

1. Enter the id's of the elements you want in your tour into the tourArray. Note : I'm assuming all of these elements will be available in the view of the body by the time it is showcased by Billy Bubbles.

2. Add the custom property data-bubble="" to your html elements on the tour with value being the content you want to show in the bubble.

3. The first element on your tour should be an invisible one postioned near the center of the landing spot, so Billy can introduce himself and the tour.

4. The last element in your tour should be/be inside the Billy start-tour button, currently in the top right corner of the page with the id "outro". This will be where Billy says bye and points out the button for the user to start the tour again.

5. Choose the colors and font you'd like for Silly Billy Bubbles and plug them into the custom CSS variables in the :root{} at the top of Billy's CSS file.

6. You can also tweak your timings and postionings in the JavaScript file.

7. Feel free to change the images and theme of the Bubbles. After all, there's only ONE Silly Billy Bubbles!
